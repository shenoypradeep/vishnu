About Vedic Space

Vedic Space is an online 'Sangha' (community) where we study and explore the practical applications of Sanatana Dharma. We bring ancient wisdom from across traditions into our modern lives, learning its significance and how to adapt it alongside like-minded seekers.

Through learning, experience sharing, and this Sādhanā app, we help each other grow and progress on our spiritual journeys.
